Middlebury Snowbowl Mapping Project

I study the geology of the northeastern United States using field mapping, geochronology, and geochemistry. My thesis focuses on the Middlebury Snowbowl — investigating whether the rocks are Grenvillian basement or rift-related cover sequences.
